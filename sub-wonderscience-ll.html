Luminous Light

Light is all around us. There are many colours of light. Think of all the colours of the rainbow! Red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo and violet. Did you know that there are some colours of light which are invisible to us?

Did you know that, without light, there would be no life on Earth? Have you ever wondered why the sky is blue?

Did you know that you are looking back in time when you look at the stars, and even when you look at the sun or the moon?

Welcome to wonderful world of light!
